The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

My vision for Bangladesh is a country where poverty is completely eradicated, and every individual has access to opportunities that allow them to thrive. I dream of a Bangladesh where social business models and microfinance empower people, especially women, to build a brighter future. I envision a society where education, healthcare, and entrepreneurship are accessible to all, fostering innovation and equality. By prioritizing social impact alongside profit, businesses can drive meaningful change. I believe Bangladesh can lead the world, demonstrating that sustainable growth and social progress can go hand-in-hand, creating a future of shared prosperity for everyone.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
